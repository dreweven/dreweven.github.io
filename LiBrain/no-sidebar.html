About Us


This is LiBrain, is a library system that was made for easier life for the librarian in STI San Pablo.
The purpose of Librain is to operate a library with efficiency. The library management system makes a library a smart one by organizing the books systematically by author, title and subject. This enables users to search for books quickly and effortlessly.
